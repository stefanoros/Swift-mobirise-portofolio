My name is Paul Swift and I am a game designer at Nintendo.

I obtained a degree in game design from the NYU Game Center, and today I work as a game designer in New York, USA. I'm passionate about producing distinctive video game artwork that appeals to the widest audience possible. I enjoy using my designs to convey stories and to make characters that all players would admire.

Since the beginning of 2019, I have been employed by Nintendo, but I have also given my freelance work my whole attention since I enjoy being "out there" and available to help anyone who requires it.
