MY PROJECTS

I'm passionate about producing distinctive video game artwork that appeals to the widest audience possible. I enjoy using my designs to convey stories and to make characters that all players would admire. Since the beginning of 2019, I have been employed by Nintendo, but I have also given my freelance work my whole attention since I enjoy being "out there" and available to help anyone who requires it. 

Below you can find some of my work, the projects I put my mind and work into from the past years.

Kitty world: Jerry's apocalypse

About the design:
For the main characters I took inspiration from the popular Tv cartoon show that everybody knows, and that is "Tom and Jerry". I wanted to make the main character: Jerry, as much human as possible and that's why I made him wearing bad-ass retro clothes. The big villains of the game are: the Kitties. I made them all look the same and even if they look adorable,like you want to pet them, like they are inoccent, the players will find out that things are not always what they seem...


About the game:
The game follows a pirate mouse, Jerry, who is looking for a secret cheese weapon to end the tirany of the rullers of the world, the Kitties.

The fairy side of the forest

About the design:
When I first started sketching for this game, I knew I had to add as much magic as possible, but also keep it close to the reality that we live in. Taking long walks into Central Park helped me to achieve a closer ideea to what I wanted the designs to look like and since the target audience for this game were kids between 7 and 12 years old, I choosed the animals in the game to have "baby" looks.


About the game:
It's like a bed-time story but put in a virtual game. Fairy-land is a mystic realm where every animal and magical beeing can find shelter in the fairy Bella's house. Everything is peacefull until one day, when the anti-hero comes and steals the book of spells from Bella, the good fairy. A hero must come in hand, or everything might be in danger...

Hazelnut treasure - part 3

About the design:
When I started designing for this game, I took a look over at one of the main characters from the popular, well known, Ice Age franchise - and that is Scrat, the squirrel who is always having troubles with collecting his beloved accorns. That's the main source of my inspiration for this game.


About the game:
Winter is coming...but this is not a regular and short winter since the dark warlock put a black spell over all the realm of Hazelnustria. Alvin and his family are in big danger but there is nothing he wouldn't do to keep his family safe. He asks for help from the White Bunny, a powerful wizard and they both go in a journey to find the hazelnut treasure, the only thing that can stop the long winter curse and keep everyone safe.
