ABOUT ME

Welcome to my world! My name is Paul Swift and I'm living my best life as a game designer. I'm delighted to apply my creative problem-solving abilities that I have developed during my studies and career in the game design industry and contribute to the creation of top-notch user experiences.

My Timeline

13th December 1989

Early life

I was born and raised in New York. Single kid, no other siblings to share my childhood with but my family made sure that I wouldn't feel lonely. My parents raised me to be a good person, to respect the others and the environment. For as long as I remember, I always had an attraction towards art and everything related to it. I loved to paint, to take pictures and to create small pieces of music. 
I followed this artistic part ever since then... 

Time flies

Hobbies

Reading. I'm having a great time just reading things in my free time. I mostly look up after books that can give me inspiration. Sci-fi books always tend to sparkle something in my brain, to make my thoughts go wild, to put my imagination at work. 

Travelling.  The world is full of wonders and after all, we only live once so I hope as long as I'm alive, I will get the chance to visit every single country of this world.

Photography. I love the fact that I can capture such wonderful moments in time. It inspires me deeply.

Present day

Studies & Work

Graduated the school of Art & Design in NYC and then I went to a college that focused on the same topics. I studied game design at the NYU Game Center and now, I'm a game designer who still lives in New York, USA and who's very passionate about creating unique artworks for video-games that can attract as many people as possible. I love to tell stories with my design and to create characters that every player will adore.

Since 2019 I started working at Nintendo but I also put my focus on the freelance part because I enjoy being "out there" in the world, being ready to come in hand is someone needs professional help.
